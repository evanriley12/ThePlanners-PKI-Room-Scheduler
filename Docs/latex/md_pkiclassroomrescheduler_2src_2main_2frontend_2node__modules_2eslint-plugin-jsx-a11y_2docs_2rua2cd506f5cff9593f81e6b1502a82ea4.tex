Enforce onmouseover/onmouseout are accompanied by onfocus/onblur. Coding for the keyboard is important for users with physical disabilities who cannot use a mouse, AT compatibility, and screenreader users.\hypertarget{md_pkiclassroomrescheduler_2src_2main_2frontend_2node__modules_2eslint-plugin-jsx-a11y_2docs_2rua2cd506f5cff9593f81e6b1502a82ea4_autotoc_md10762}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Rule options}{Rule options}}\label{md_pkiclassroomrescheduler_2src_2main_2frontend_2node__modules_2eslint-plugin-jsx-a11y_2docs_2rua2cd506f5cff9593f81e6b1502a82ea4_autotoc_md10762}
