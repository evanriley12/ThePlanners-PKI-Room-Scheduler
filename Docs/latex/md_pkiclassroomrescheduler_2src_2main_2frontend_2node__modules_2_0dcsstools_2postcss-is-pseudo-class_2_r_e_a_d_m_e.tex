{\ttfamily \+:is} takes the specificity of the most specific list item. We can increase specificity with {\ttfamily \+:not} selectors, but we can\textquotesingle{}t decrease it.

Converted selectors are ensured to have the same specificity as {\ttfamily \+:is} for the most important bit. Less important bits can have higher specificity that {\ttfamily \+:is}.
